Project Overview

JFKReveal is a cutting-edge tool that analyzes declassified documents from the National Archives to uncover evidence about the JFK assassination using advanced AI techniques and Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG). By processing thousands of documents and applying deep analytical methods, we aim to bring clarity to one of history's most controversial events.
