Key JFK Events Timeline

Tracing the critical moments before and after November 22, 1963

Oct 1962

Cuban Missile Crisis

Kennedy confronts the Soviet Union over nuclear missiles in Cuba, bringing the world to the brink of nuclear war.

Nov 21, 1963

Arrival in Texas

President Kennedy arrives in Texas for a political trip that includes stops in San Antonio, Houston, Fort Worth, and Dallas.

Nov 22, 1963

Assassination

President Kennedy is assassinated while riding in a motorcade through Dealey Plaza in Dallas, Texas at 12:30 PM CST.

Nov 24, 1963

Oswald Murdered

Lee Harvey Oswald is shot and killed by Jack Ruby while being transferred from the city jail to the county jail.

Sep 1964

Warren Report

The Warren Commission releases its report, concluding that Lee Harvey Oswald acted alone in assassinating President Kennedy.

1976-1979

House Investigation

The House Select Committee on Assassinations concludes that Kennedy was "probably assassinated as a result of a conspiracy."
